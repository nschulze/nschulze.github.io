The Event

MDA Muscle Team is unlike any event in Central Iowa. Our high profile sports themed dinner and auction attracts local and nationally known athletes who volunteer to flex their muscles alongside local MDA Youth Ambassadors. Together, the athletes and ambassadors create the Muscle Team. This event gives our youth ambassadors, whose muscle disease may limit their ability, to be a part of a sports team, the once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to be on a team with top athletes. The Muscle Team's mission - to put muscle behind MDA's dedicated quest to find treatments and cures for neuromuscular disease.

MDA of Iowa

The Muscular Dystrophy Association is the world's leading nonprofit health agency dedicated to finding treatments and cures for muscular dystrophy, amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) and other neuromuscular diseases. We do so by funding worldwide research; by providing comprehensive health care services and support to MDA families nationwide; and by rallying communities to fight back through advocacy, fundraising and local engagement. It's special work powered by special people who give generously.

MDA of Iowa covers 88 counties in Iowa and Western Illinois serving over 1600 families affected by one of 43 neuromuscular diseases. Services include research, clinic management and payment assistance, medical equipment loans and repairs, support groups, youth summer camp and flu shots. $.77 of every dollar raised goes towards funding our mission and stays local to help families in Iowa and Western Illinois.
